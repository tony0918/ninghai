穹景体育

穹景体育成立于2014年初，通过体育赛事，来挑战和挖掘个人潜能，并传递自由、冒险、激情、竞争，以及热爱运动的精神。穹景致力于提供创新、有价值的体育赛事，使参与者通过赛事达到个人目标、享受乐趣，并得到激励。我们在环保与公益领域也不断进行积极探索，鼓励选手参与以回馈社会。

宁海越野挑战赛

第一届宁海越野挑战赛于2013年10月5日举行，赛程50公里，83人报名参赛，67人完赛。第一届男女冠军分别是来自上海的卢华强和台州的方美平。赛事当天浓雾弥漫，导致多名选手途中迷路。

第二届宁海越野挑战赛于2014年9月21日举行，赛道后半程因为台风大雨预警而改道，实际赛程45公里。329人报名，实际出发294人，288人完赛。第二届男女冠军分别是来自Salomon国际队队员闫龙飞（目前穹景体育的员工）和来自上海的马妍星。

第三届宁海越野挑战赛于2015年10月18日举行，赛程50公里，比赛起点更换到星海小学。共有来自十一个国家地区的600人报名，实际出发457人，409人完赛。第三届男女冠军分别是宁海本地选手潘宗祥和来自宁波的史红霞。

“宁海越野挑战赛”已经走过三年时光，三年的累积和成长已经使“宁海越野挑战赛”成为中国大陆地区较为成熟的中距离越野比赛之一。我们寄望“宁海越野挑战赛”继续发展，能让更多人参与其中，经过审慎评估以及和当地政府的多次沟通，决定从2016年开始做以下调整：

每年分春季赛和秋季赛；

2016年宁海越野挑战赛秋季赛定于10月15日举行，设置两个组别：100公里、累积爬升4800米，42公里、累积爬升1900米。2016年秋季赛两个组别的起点均为宁海城区西门城楼，100公里途经浙东大峡谷的白溪水库，终点位于干坑；42公里终点位于浙东大峡谷的白溪水库。

2017年春季赛将于2017年4月举行，设50公里、24公里和11公里家庭亲子三个组别，起点均为星海小学。50公里终点在冯家，24公里在许家山折返沿环线回到星海小学，11公里家庭亲子终点在许家山。

宁海越野挑战赛”秋季赛英文名称为 "Ultra-Trail Ninghai", 春季赛英文名称为 “Ninghai Trail”，赛事logo相同，赛事网站均为 www.ultratrailninghai.com

Ninghai Trail and Ultra-Trail Ninghai are organized by Skyview Sports.

Skyview Sports was established at the beginning of 2014.

Skyview Sports helps people to challenge and dig into their potential energy, while transmitting spirit of freedom, adventure, enthusiasm, competition and passion for sports through endurance events. Also provides innovative and valuable sport race, which enables each individual to achieve personal success and gain interest from it, and to be inspired by it. To approach environment and charity fields further, encouraging participation to connect the community.

Skyview Sports also organizes Nanjing Mountain Marathon and The Thousand Island Lake Marathon. And will continue bringing you more brilliant races in the year of 2016 and in the future.

Race combines Ninghai Trail in Spring and Ultra-Trail Ninghai in Autumn. Both competitions will take place at the stunning backdrop of the national mountain fitness trail in Ninghai in 2016.

Our goal with races is twofold: to create a race that will enter into sporting legend, while also respond to the increasing popularity of both race and giving newcomers the opportunity to have a go.

The Spring Ninghai Trail, which includes 50km, 24km and 11km family run, will provides equal challenge and opportunity for the most discerning Trail runner and the newcomer respectively.

The Autumn Ultra-Trail Ninghai, hosted in the western mountainous area of Ninghai, will challenge the most talented competitors in 100km group, while also allows the less-experienced to perform at a level in 42km group they are comfortable with on courses that are marked out and made safe for the event.

Ninghai National Mountain Fitness Trail, the most popular trail in China, is a Ninghai icon in itself. Encircled by 500km trail with many mountains, it is without doubt the ideal venue for a competition that aims to set a new standard of Trail Running by attracting the largest number of competitors and spectators.
