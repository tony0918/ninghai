宁海越野挑战赛

第一届宁海越野挑战赛于2013年10月5日举行，赛程50公里，83人报名参赛，67人完赛。第一届男女冠军分别是来自上海的卢华强和台州的方美平。赛事当天浓雾弥漫，导致多名选手途中迷路。

第二届宁海越野挑战赛于2014年9月21日举行，赛道后半程因为台风大雨预警而改道，实际赛程45公里。329人报名，实际出发294人，288人完赛。第二届男女冠军分别是来自Salomon国际队队员闫龙飞（目前穹景体育的员工）和来自上海的马妍星。

第三届宁海越野挑战赛于2015年10月18日举行，赛程50公里，比赛起点更换到星海小学。共有来自十一个国家地区的600人报名，实际出发457人，409人完赛。第三届男女冠军分别是宁海本地选手潘宗祥和来自宁波的史红霞。

第四届宁海越野挑战赛于2016年10月15日举行，设置两个组别：100公里组，42公里组。两个组别的起点均为宁海城区西门城楼，100公里途经浙东大峡谷的白溪水库，终点位于乾溪山庄；42公里终点位于浙东大峡谷的白溪水库。总计829人报名，289人选择了100公里组，540人选择了42公里组。100公里组男女冠军分别是闫正庆和徐林菊，42公里组男女冠军分别是楼荣伟和东丽。

“宁海越野挑战赛”已经走过四年时光，四年的累积和成长已经使“宁海越野挑战赛”成为中国大陆地区较为成熟的中长距离越野赛之一。我们寄望“宁海越野挑战赛”继续发展，能让更多人参与其中，经过审慎评估以及和当地政府的多次沟通，决定从2017年开始做以下调整：

每年分春季赛和秋季赛；

2017年春季赛将于2017年3月举行，设50公里和11.5公里家庭组两个组别，起点均为星海小学。50公里终点在冯家， 11.5公里家庭组终点在许家山。

Ultra-Trail Ninghai and Ninghai Classic are organized by Skyview Sports.

Skyview Sports was established at the beginning of 2014.Skyview Sports helps people to challenge and dig into their potential energy, while transmitting spirit of freedom, adventure, enthusiasm, competition and passion for sports through endurance events. Also provides innovative and valuable sport race, which enables each individual to achieve personal success and gain interest from it, and to be inspired by it. To approach environment and charity fields further, encouraging participation to connect the community.

Skyview Sports also organizes Nanjing Mountain Marathon and The Thousand Island Lake Marathon. Race combines Ninghai Classic and Ultra-Trail Ninghai.

Our goal with races is twofold: to create a race that will enter into sporting legend, while also respond to the increasing popularity of both race and giving newcomers the opportunity to have a go.

Ninghai Classic, which includes 50km and 11.5km family run, will provides equal challenge and opportunity for the most discerning Trail runner and the newcomer respectively.

The Ultra-Trail Ninghai, hosted in the western mountainous area of Ninghai, will challenge the most talented competitors in 100km group, while also allows the less-experienced to perform at a level in 42km group they are comfortable with on courses that are marked out and made safe for the event.

Ninghai National Mountain Fitness Trail, the most popular trail in China, is a Ninghai icon in itself. Encircled by 500km trail with many mountains, it is without doubt the ideal venue for a competition that aims to set a new standard of Trail Running by attracting the largest number of competitors and spectators.
