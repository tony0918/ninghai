有关赛事 RACE PROFILE

赛道位置

赛道坐落于玉龙雪山南侧，以玉龙雪山经典徒步线路为主。起点设于距丽江古城10公里的著名景点拉市海湿地公园，线路途经文海、丽江高山植物园、玉龙第一峰、玉柱擎天及玉龙大峡谷，终点设置于玉龙雪山脚下的原始村落——玉湖村。赛道整体海拔处于2400m-4460m之间，既有简单的土路和高山小径，也包含有一定技术难度的高山碎坡，赛道风光糅合了冰蚀湖、高山草甸、雪域冰川、原始森林在内的多重景观。无与伦比的高山赛道，迷人的雪域风光，一望无际的开阔视野，尽在2017玉龙雪山超级越野赛。

赛事组别

赛事包括两个组别——超级越野跑50km和天空竞速赛21km两个组别，赛道整体海拔在2400m-4460m之间，包括两段较大的爬升和一定比例的技术路段，两个组别的起点分别为拉市海湿地公园和玉柱擎天，终点均设于玉湖村。

奖项设置

两个组别的男女前三都将获得由赞助商提供的奖品。
所有完赛选手，均可获得设计精美的完赛纪念品。

Location

Race course is along the southern side of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain, covering the classic hiking route in this area. The course starts from Lashi Lake Wetland Park, which is 10 kilometers away from the old town of Lijiang, then passing Culture Sea, Lijiang Alpine Botanical Garden, Yulong Peak, Skyreach Pilla and Yulong Grand Canyon, and ends in Jade Lake village which is an ancient village located at the foot of Yulong Snow Mountain. The average altitude is between 2,400m and 4,460m. The course is a mix of off road, single track and rugged slope with certain technical difficulties. Scenes along the course are varied: glacial erosion lake, alpine meadow, and virgin forest. Incomparable mountain track, stunning snow scenery, great horizons, all you will discover from Lijiang SkyView Adventure 2017.

Race Distance

There are two race distances: Ultra 50K and Sky running 21K. The average altitude is between 2,400m and 4,460m. There are two big climbs and certain technical terrains. 50K start from Lashi Lake Wetland Park and 21K start from Skyreach Pilla. Both finish at Jade Lake village.

Awards

Top 3 of each distance will be granted awards provided by sponsors. Runners who finish the race within cut-off time will receive a nice completion souvenir.
