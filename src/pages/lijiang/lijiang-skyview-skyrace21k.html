赛事规则

环保

禁止随意丢弃垃圾，请使用垃圾袋带走所有个人垃圾。请勿惊扰沿途的野生动物或破坏野生植物。另外，组委会在比赛组织过程中将尽可能使用可回收、可复用材料以减少资源浪费（电池、纸杯、塑料等）。
比赛结束后，组委会将立即对赛道上布置的路标和垃圾进行清理。

物品寄存

每位参赛选手可于比赛开始前在起点处寄存一个包（容量不超过30L），请将组委会发放的存包贴纸粘贴于包外指定位置并清晰标明参赛号码等相关信息。比赛开始后，组委会将安排将所有存包转运至比赛终点玉湖村。

号码布与计时芯片

比赛过程中，所有选手的号码布必需置于身体前侧显著位置并清晰地露出参赛号码，请勿对号码布进行折叠。此外，备用的计时芯片请挂载于背包外侧显著位置。 号码布仅限本人参赛使用，严禁任何形式的号码布转让。

补给站与关门时间信息

赛道上将设置若干补给点，补给品类型包括：水、运动饮料、风味小吃、甜食、干果、水果、热食等多种补给。
此外，每个补给站都设有计时点，部分计时点将设置关门时间。组委会有权在赛事期间对关门时间进行适当调整。一旦选手超出关门时间抵达/离开补给站，组委会将取消该选手参赛资格并取回号码布和计时芯片。关门时间及补给类型如下：

强制装备和建议装备

所有选手必需全程携带以下所有强制装备，建议装备可根据比赛当天情况选择性携带。组委会将在赛道中的计时点随机设置强制装备检查点，一旦发现选手出现强制装备缺失的情况，将严格按照<罚时与取消比赛资格>中的规定进行处理。

强制装备：
手机：充满电，比赛过程中保持全程开机
急救毯：尺寸至少为1.2m*2m
救生哨：越野背包上的救生哨或类似产品，需确保能够正常使用
医用绷带：尺寸至少为7.5cm*2.3m
防水夹克：带帽、全压胶、防水透气，GORE-TEX及类似面料产品均可（塑料雨衣、防风夹克及抗水 (Water Resistant) 夹克均不被认可。）

个人水具：总容量至少1L以上
便携水杯：出于环保考虑，补给站将不提供任何纸杯
保暖上衣：羊毛、抓绒、棉服或羽绒制品均可
长裤：具备良好的防风性能
保暖帽：需要覆盖整个头部，魔术头巾不被认可
手套：防风保暖，建议携带有填充材料的手套
能量食品：所含热量至少200千卡

退出比赛

主动退赛
除在比赛中遭遇严重伤病或其他意外情况的选手之外，选手只能在补给站退出比赛。
退赛后选手可以在补给站休息，并等候接驳车辆/马匹将选手运送至比赛终点。
强制退赛
经医护人员判断，如选手当前的情况可能危及到选手的人身安全时，组委会有权强制要求该选手暂时停止比赛或退出比赛。

天气状况

如果在比赛中出现恶劣天气或其他不可抗力因素，组委会保留取消比赛、终止比赛或对补给点、赛道进行临时修改的权利。如果出现以上情况，不予退还报名费。

罚时与取消比赛资格

未能正确佩戴号码布：罚时15分钟
随地乱扔垃圾：罚时1小时
离开路标完好的赛道超过500m：罚时15分钟
在比赛过程中抄近道：取消比赛资格
缺失强制装备1件：罚时1小时
缺失强制装备超过1件或逃避强制装备检查：取消比赛资格
错过任意一个计时点：取消比赛资格
超出关门时间：取消比赛资格
个人退出比赛且不告知主办方：禁止参加主办方今后的比赛

Regulation

Environment

Please do not leave any trash along the course. Use your own trash bag to carry anything you want to throw away. Please do not disturb wild animals or destroy wild plants along the course. Recycled materials will be used as much as possible to reduce negative impact on environment around the race area. Race organizer will clean up all course markers and trash I race area right after the race is closed.

Bag deposit

Each runner can deposit one race bag at the start, with maximum capacity of 30L. Please write down your race number on the sticky paper in your race bag and put it at the outside of your deposit bag. We will transport all deposit bags to Jade Lake village where race is finished.

Race number and timing chip

Please wear race number at a visible place in front of your body during the race. Please do not fold it. The backup timing chip is suggested to tie to your bag at a visible place. Race number cannot be transferred to other people.

Aid station and cut-off time

Several aids stations will be set along the course. Food provided at aid stations include water, sports drink, coffee, snack, sweet good, nuts, fruit, and hot food.

All aid stations have timing point. A few stations will have their own cut-off time. Race organizer reserves the right to adjust all cut-off times during the race in order to ensure the safety of all participants. If you leave aid station after its cut-off time, you will be disqualified. Timing chip will be taken back. Cut-off times and suplies provided at aid stations are：

Location Distance completed Accumulated elevation gain Aid station Cut-off time
Start – Skyreach Pilla 0 0 Water N/A
CP1 – Leech dam 5.2k 620m Water, sports drink 2h
CP2 – Leech dam 13.5k 1,750m Water, sports drink, coffee, hot food N/A

Mandatory gears and recommend gears

Competitors must carry all mandatory gear listed as below. As for recommend gears, you can choose what to carry with you depending on the actual weather condition on race day. Organizer will conduct random checks on mandatory gears during the race. Rules list in <Penalty and disqualification> will be applied if competitor not carrying all mandatory gears.

Quit

Quit the race voluntarily
Competitors can withdraw from the race at any aid station, unless having serious illness or any other unexpected emergencies. Those who have withdrawn from the race shall wait at aid station for shuttle bus or horse to transport you back to the finish.

Forced to quit
If you are considered of experiencing risks which would impact your personal safety by medical staff, race organizer reserve the rights to stop you from the race.

Weather

If race encounters severe weather condition or any force majeure happens during the race, race organizer reserve the rights to cancel the race, stop the race or place emergency measures on aid stations and course. If above situation occurs, no refund for registration fee will be provided.

Penalty and disqualification

Not wearing race number properly: 15 min penalty
Throwing trash away: 1 h penalty
Leaving well marked course over 500m: 15 min penalty
Taking short cut: disqualification
Lacking of one piece of compulsory gears: 1 h penalty
Lacking of over one piece of compulsory gears and avoiding gear check: disqualification
Missing a timing point: disqualification
Arrive after cut-off time: disqualification
Quit without notifying race organizer: banned from future races
