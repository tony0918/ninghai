赛道 COURSE

宁海越野挑战赛春季赛事在浙江省宁波市宁海县举行，赛道途经竹林、多为狭窄山路，极其陡峭的磨注峰是比赛最具挑战的部分。比赛饱览自然风光，并极具越野风格。

Ninghai Classic is a unique event held Spring in Ninghai County, Ningbo, Zhejiang province. It aims to challenge your inner spirit and physical state, as well as to provide race participants with a magnificent view of natural scene and environment.

Most of the route is tough mountainous terrain and part of the route goes through Ninghai national mountain fitness trail. Participants will be running through bamboo forest and rural back roads. The most challenging part is the steep hill of Mozhu Peak at an altitude of 872m.
