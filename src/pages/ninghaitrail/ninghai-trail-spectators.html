观众支援 Support Crew

我们鼓励选手的家属或朋友为选手去终点加油，但是因为交通和组织的限制，强烈不建议驾驶私家车。

我们安排城区到50公里组终点冯家村的来回巴士。

具体的班次安排详见交通住宿：http://www.ultratrailninghai.com/#/ninghai-trail/transportation-and-hotel

 

Support from runner's relatives and friends are highly appreciated and warmly welcomed. Due to the transportation limitation, self driving to finish line to provide support is not suggested.

Organizer will arrange shuttle buses assisting support crew to travel: From the Urban to the finish of 50km.

You can find the details of the Shuttle Bus Schedule in TRANSPORTATION AND HOTEL： http://www.ultratrailninghai.com/#/ninghai-trail/transportation-and-hotel
