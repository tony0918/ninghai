交通住宿 TRANSPORTATION AND HOTEL

1、抵达宁海
a) 火车：上海、杭州、宁波等主要城市均有火车可抵达宁海；
b) 飞机：距离宁海最近的机场是宁波机场，到达宁波机场后可换乘火车或巴士到达宁海；
c) 巴士：宁波、杭州均有巴士可到达宁海。
2、抵达赛事包发放地点和赛事起点
赛事包发放地点和赛事起点均在宁海县城区的星海小学，步行或打车均可到达。

1. To Ninghai
a) By train: Trains are available from Shanghai, Hangzhou and Ningbo to Ninghai County.
b) By plane: The nearest airport is Ningbo Airport. From there, train or bus is available to Ninghai County.
c) By bus: Bus is available from Ningbo, Hangzhou to Ninghai County.
2. To the Race Packet Pickup, and Start
The Race Packet pickup and Race Start are all located at Xinghai Primary School. You can get there on foot or take taxi.
