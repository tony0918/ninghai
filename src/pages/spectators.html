观众支援 Support Crew

我们鼓励选手的家属或朋友为选手去沿途和终点加油，但是因为交通和组织的限制，强烈不建议驾驶私家车。

我们安排起点到42公里组终点白溪水库的来回巴士、起点到CP6冠峰的来回巴士、CP6冠峰到100公里终点乾溪山庄的巴士、起点到100公里终点乾溪山庄的来回巴士。

具体的班次安排、车票预订会尽快公布。

 

Support from runner's relatives and friends are highly appreciated and warmly welcomed. Due to the transportation limitation, self driving to any point to provide support is not suggested.

Organizer will arrange shuttle buses assisting support crew to travel:

  • From the start to the finish of 42KM
  • From the start to CP6,
  • From CP6 to the finish,
  • From the start to the finish of 100KM

Details of shuttle bus arrangement and how to purchase tickets will be released soon.
