交通住宿 TRANSPORTATION AND HOTEL

1、抵达宁海
a) 火车：从上海、杭州、宁波等重要城市有火车可以抵达宁海
b) 飞机：最近的机场是宁波机场，然后从宁波换乘火车或巴士到宁海
c) 巴士：从宁波、杭州有巴士到宁海
2、抵达赛事包发放地点、展览会和赛事起点
赛事包发放地点、展览会和赛事起点都在宁海县城区的西门城楼，步行或出租车可以抵达
3、离开赛事终点
a) 赛事组织方将在100公里组和42公里组终点提供交通协助，穿梭巴士将往返于从终点到宁海城区;
b) 选手无需承担费用

1、To Ninghai
a) By train: Trains available from Shanghai, Hangzhou and Ningbo to Ninghai County.
b) By plane: The nearest airport is Ningbo Airport. From there, train or bus available to Ninghai County.
c) By bus: Bus available from Ningbo, Hangzhou to Ninghai County.
2、To the Race Packet Pickup, Expo and Start
The Race Packet pickup, race expo and Race Start are all located at West Gate, which is within walking distance with major hotels in Ninghai County. Or you can take a taxi to get there.
3、After the Race
Transportation service between the Race Finish of 100km/42km and Ninghai County will be provided by race organizer with free charge.
