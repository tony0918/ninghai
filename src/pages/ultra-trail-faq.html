常见问题 FAQs

问题 1：
相比2016年宁海越野挑战赛，今年比赛变化在哪些地方？

答案：
a) 赛事成为UTWT的一部分，是Discovery Race一站；
b) 42公里组别后半段做调整，距离变为50公里组别（实际53.1公里），终点和100公里组别一样到乾溪山庄；
c) 100公里组别赛道路线不变，但中间一段方向更改，能让选手白天经过风景优美的清水溪、混水溪；
d) 50公里组别取消装备检查，关门时间到12个小时；
e) 允许所有选手使用登山杖；
f) 为了让更多越野跑爱好者参与赛事，除了年龄限制，不做其它任何报名参赛限制，且报名费做调整。

问题 2：
比赛出发时间早上6点，参赛选手应该如何前往起点？

答案：
赛事起点位于宁海县西门城楼，组委会将安排接驳车，分别从宁海城区的三个地点（【开元新世纪酒店】、【世贸中心大酒店】、【莫泰酒店大观文化园公交车站】）将选手运送至比赛起点。
接驳车辆时间及班次安排，赛前详见：http://www.skyviewsport.com/#/transportation-and-hotel 。由于接驳车的运载能力有限，未能坐上车的参赛选手需要自行前往起点。(注意：因为宁海的出租车数量有限，推荐使用滴滴快车)。

问题 3：
参赛选手的亲友是否能够去终点迎接选手，该如何前往？

答案：
参赛选手的亲友可以去到终点迎接选手，组委会将提供从起点至50&100公里组终点免费接驳服务，请密切注意车辆的出发时间。接驳车辆时间及班次安排，赛前详见：http://www.skyviewsport.com/#/transportation-and-hotel 。

问题 4：终点距城区的距离怎么样，选手该如何回到城区，组委会有摆渡车吗？

答案：
终点距城区约30分钟车程。组委会将安排接驳车接送选手回到城区，接驳车辆时间及班次安排，赛前详见：http://www.skyviewsport.com/#/transportation-and-hotel。晚上抵达100公里组终点的选手，组委会也提供选手终点休息场地。

问题 5：
退赛的流程是怎样的，怎样才能拿到自己的存衣包？

答案：
选手只能在补给点退赛。退赛流程：先联系补给站计时裁判（非计时点请联系补给站站长），确认退赛并在号码布上盖章。然后在补给站休息调整，等待组委会的车辆将选手接往终点领取自己的存衣包（无法获得完赛奖牌及马甲）。
如果无法及时回到终点领取赛事包，可以在10月15日中午12:00－12:30到起点西门城楼领取存衣包。

问题 6：
什么时间在哪儿领取赛事包？需要携带什么东西领取？赛事包中都有哪些东西？

答案：
赛事包的领取时间为2017年10月13日(星期五)下午13:00-21:00，领取地点：宁海县徐霞客大道西门城楼。所有选手领取赛事包时需携带自己的身份证或护照原件（报名时所使用的证件），100公里组选手需携带强制装备。
赛事包中包括以下条目：号码布1张、备用芯片1只（含说明书与扎带）、别针4只、存衣包2个（50公里组1个）、存衣包贴纸2张（50公里组1张）、速干赛事T恤1件、救生毯1份、以及其它赞助商物品。
除了比赛前一天可以在宁海西门城楼领取，还能提前一周在特定城市的Salomon门店领取，但仅限报名时选择该项服务的选手。

问题 7：
2017宁海越野挑战赛有积分吗，每个组别多少积分？

答案：
本次比赛将申请ITRA赛事积分.100公里组完赛选手将获得5个积分，50公里组完赛选手将获得3个积分。

问题 8：
比赛期间宁海的天气和温度的大致情况怎样，会很冷吗？

答案：
宁海10月份的天气不太稳定，有一定降雨的可能性。从往年的天气数据上看，最低气温基本在10-15度之间，早上出发会比较凉一些。如果下雨，体感温度会进一步降低。所以请所有选手做好保暖准备，以保证自身安全。

问题 9：
领取装备需要打印领物单吗？

答案：
选手无需自行打印领物单，组委会将准备好领物单及免责声明，在确认身份之后发放给选手，随后前往强制装备检查并领取赛事包。

问题 10：
我因伤无法前来参加今年的比赛，该如何领取参赛包？能让人代领吗？

答案：
赛事包需本人携带身份证原件方能领取，禁止任何形式的代领。

问题 11：
前往起点及终点返回城区是否方便打车?

答案：
因为宁海的出租车数量有限，推荐使用滴滴快车,可以方便前往起点及从终点返回。

Q1: What’s new for this year compared with 2016 edition?

A:
a) UTNH is selected as one of Discovery Races under UTWT.
b) Second half of 50km has minor change, Total distance is extended to 53.1km.which will be finished same as 100km at Qian Xi Villa.
c) Course of 100km remains the same except one section switching direction. Competitors will be able to enjoy beautiful scenery of Qingshui creek and Hunshui creek in daytime.
d) No compulsory gear check for 50km. Cut-off time for 50km is extended to 12hours.
e) Trekking poles are allowed to use for all competitors.
f) Other than age restriction, there will be no any other restriction for registration so as to attract more people to be involved. Registration fee will be adjust.

Q2: Race will start at 6am. How do we get to the start point?

A:
Race start is West Gate of Ninghai County, four free shuttle buses will be arranged by race organizer to pick up runners who’d like to transport from their hotels by bus to race start. There will be three pick-up venues: kaiyuan New Century Hotel, World Trade Center Hotel, motel168 hotel. Bus schedule can be found at http://www.skyviewsport.com/#/transportation-and-hotel . Seats are limited. Please arrange transportation by yourself if you can’t catch up any of our shuttle buses. Didi APP is recommended to call on a taxi.

Q3: Can relatives and friends support go to finish line to welcome competitors? How do they get there?

A:
Relatives and friends can welcome competitors at finish line. Free shuttle bus will be provided by race organizer to transport people from start line to finish line of 50km and 100km. Please pay attention to bus schedule, which can be found at http://www.skyviewsport.com/#/transportation-and-hotel.

Q4: How far is from finish line to Ninghai County? How do we get back? Do you provide shuttle bus?

A:
The finish is apart from the city about 30 minutes by car .Pubic transportation nearby finish line of 50km and 100km finish is limited. Free shuttle bus to transport competitors back to downtown will be provided by race organizer. Please pay attention to bus schedule, which can be found at http://www.skyviewsport.com/#/transportation-and-hotel. The organizing committee also provide athletes finish rest area

Q5: How do I withdraw for the race? How can I get back my deposit bag?

A:
Competitors can withdraw at aid stations. Please advise the judge or station director of your decision. Bib number will be chopped with official withdrawn stamp. Bus provide by race organizer will transport you back to finish line where you can get back your deposit bag. There will b no medal or finish souvenir for withdrawers. If you are unable to pick up deposit bag at finsh line, 12:00-12:30 on October 15th at West Gate is also good.

Q6: When and where to pick up my race packet? What do I need to present? What’s in race packet?

A:
All race packets will be distributed from 13:00 to 21:00 on Friday October 13th 2017. Address is West Gate of Xujiake Road, Ninghai county. Original ID or passport in accordance with the documentation at registration shall be presented. All compulsory gears for 100km competitors shall be presented for gear check.
Race packet includes one bib number, one backup time chip with instruction and wrapping lace, 4 pins, two deposit bags (one for 50km) and two stickers (one for 50km), one race t-shirt, one survival blanket and other commodities provided by sponsors.
In addition to pick up race packet one day prior to race day at West Gate in Ninghai donwtown, competitor can also choose to pick it up at Salomon stores in designated city one week before the race. Competitor shall indicate the preference when registration. But only for choosing the service of the player.

Q7: Does 2017 UTNH offer points? How many points for each distance?

A:
2017 UTNH will apply for ITRA point. 5 points for 100km. 3 points for 50km.

Q8: How is the weather and temperature in Ninghai during race days? Will it be cold then?

A:
Weather condition in October in Ninghai is not stable. There’s possibility of raining. From weather data over the past years, the lowest temperature is 10 - 15 degree. Early morning would be chilly. Body temperature will be cold if raining. Please get ready to keep warm for personal safety.

Competitors do not need to print notice to pick up race packet. Information sheet and disclaimer will be prepared by race organizer and will be given to competitors when identification confirmed. Compulsory check and race packet pick-up is coming after.
