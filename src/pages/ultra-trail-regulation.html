赛事规则 REGULATION

环保

  1. 必须沿标注路线依靠自身能力完成比赛，全程不允许使用手杖。
  2. 保护沿途野生动植物和环境。
  3. 请勿随意丢弃垃圾，请使用赛事包中提供的封口垃圾袋放置需要丢弃的垃圾。

号码簿

  1. 比赛过程中请将号码薄置于身体前侧显著位置，避免号码薄被背包带或衣物遮挡。选手凭号码薄可乘坐组委会免费穿梭巴士、进出各补给站、存取寄存包，若选手未遵守赛事规则，组委会有权停用其号码簿。组委会紧急联系电话已印于号码簿上，建议选手将个人信息及紧急联络人信息写于号码簿背面。
  2. 参赛者必须按照标注路线行进，不允许擅自改道或抄近路。如果迷路，请按原路返回至上一个可见的赛道标示，辨明方向后再继续前进。

安全

  1. 越野跑比赛距离较长，每位选手需要对自身安危负责。所有强制装备是为了选手安全，并确保选手在赛道偏僻不方便到达区域时的基本所需。请了解并熟悉耐力运动中一些危险体征的后果，如脱水、低温症和极度疲劳。赛前请密切留意天气预报以便应对。比赛期间赛道不对公众封闭，请尊重路段遇到的游人等。
  2. 急救
    赛事组织方将于各检查站补给站及终点提供力所能及的医疗急救支持，如果途中需要紧急救助，请拨打号码薄上的紧急联系电话与组委会联系，组委会将组织沿途急救人员寻找求救选手并提供协助。
    如因非赛事组织方过失发生不可抗力事由在内的危险情况致使参赛者在比赛过程中受到身体伤害，赛后将尽最大努力提供帮助及救援，但赛事组织方不承担由此给参赛者带来的任何伤害之直接或间接的赔偿责任。本条款同样适用于在比赛过程中提供服务的志愿者。
  3. 退赛
    如果途中退赛，请前往距离最近的检查点/补给站，告知工作人员/志愿者，并签署退赛声明和剪除号码，之后等组委会安排车辆接回终点。
    若选手中途自行中止比赛并未告知组委会，将被视为弃赛，组委会不承担其之后的任何后果。
  4. 保险
    赛事组织方将为所有选手和志愿者购买比赛期间的个人意外保险。

完赛

各组别完赛选手会获得一件完赛马甲。各组别男女前三名将获得奖品。赛后3天内选手可以登录官网，并自行打印下载成绩证书。

终点交通安排

组委会将在各组别终点分别安排免费巴士，协助选手返回宁海城区，请选手凭号码薄免费乘坐。车辆安排将在赛前公布。

赛事取消

如果遇到超出赛事组织方合理控制范围的、不可避免或者无法克服的事件，如地震、台风、强暴雨、水灾等其它不可抗力之情形，赛事组织者保留取消比赛、赛事延期或改变比赛路线的权利。如果比赛因不可抗力被取消，赛事组织者将向参赛者退还扣除已发生的费用后的部分参赛费用。

Environmental Protection

  1. You must complete the marked course on foot under your own power. Trekking poles are prohibited.
  2. Please help protect wild animals and plants and our nature.
  3. Please use the zip lock bag for personal rubbish provided in race packet during the race. Littering is prohibited.

Race Bib

  1. Your race bib must be visible at outside of your clothing all time and in front of your body. Do not cover your race bib with any backpack strap or clothing. Race bib is your ticket to take free shuttle bus at the Finish, to enter all aid stations to enjoy all sorts of service and to get your deposited bag back. Breaking any of the event rules may incur disqualification. Emergency contact number is printed on the race bib. We recommend you to write down your basic info and the emergency contact person's info on the back of the race bib for safety consideration.
  2. You must stay on the marked course. Short cuts are prohibited. If you get lost, please return to the last sighted marking.

Mandatory Gear and Recommended Gear

  1. All of your mandatory gear must be carried with you for the full duration of the race. Organizer will conduct random checks on mandatory gears during the race and at the Race Finish. If anyone found of missing any item of mandatory gear during the race, he/she will be stopped and disqualified. If anyone found of missing any item of mandatory gear when arriving at the Race Finish, he/she will be disqualified.

    Mandatory Gear List for 100K distance
    1. 1 long sleeve thermal.

    4. 1 backup headlamp / torch in case of headlamp failure but you still have enough light for you to walk by and see course markings
    5. 1 mobile phone with battery fully charged.
    6. 1 whistle.
    7. 1 survival blanket or equivalent.
    8. 1 compressed bandage with minimum stretched dimensions 7.5cm wide x 2.3m long.

    14. 1 race bib, please wear it on your front and visible at all times (provided in race packet). Elastic waist strap is recommended.

Safety

  1. This is a long distance event. Each runner is ultimately responsible for their own wellbeing. The mandatory gear requirements are for your safety and are the bare minimum that you will need in remote areas that not easily accessible. Please be aware of the effects of dehydration, hypothermia, and extreme fatigue. Please check forecasts and be prepared for all conditions. The course will be open to public during the race time. Please be aware of tourists and other walkers on the trails.
  2. First Aid
    Medical emergency aid will be provided by the organizer at the Start, aid stations, checkpoints and the Finish. Please contact the emergency number printed on the race bib for help when needed.
    If participant get injured in dangerous accident caused by unpredictable and irresistible force, the race organizer will do our best to provide rescue and assistance after the race, but will not bear direct or indirect liability for compensation. This is also applied for the volunteers.
  3. Withdrawn
    If drop out of the race on the course, please go to the nearest check point. A withdrawal statement is to be signed and your race bib will be cut off. Vehicle service will be provided by the organizer to assist you to return to the Finish.
    If you drop out of the race on the course without notifying anybody, organizer will not take any responsibility for all subsequences.
  4. Insurance
    Individual accident insurance for all runners and volunteers covering race days will be purchased by the organizer.

Finisher

All finishers of two distances will receive Finisher Vest. Prizes will be granted to the top three females and males of two distances. Finisher Certificate can be downloaded and printed within three days from the race official website after the race.

Shuttle Bus Service at the Finish

Free shuttle bus service will be provided by the organizer at the Finish of two distances to assist runners to get back to Ninghai. Please present your race bib to get on the bus. Details of shuttle bus service will be released prior to the race.

Race Cancellation

Race organizer has the authority to cancel the race, postpone the race or change the race route if threatening weather conditions or irresistible cause exist, such as earthquake, tornado, thunderstorm, flood, etc. If threatening weather conditions and irresistible cause force cancellation of the race, partial refund will be provided since some funds were already spent in preparation for the race.
